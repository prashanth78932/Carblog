Nissan GT-R

The Nissan GT-R is a sports car that is made to perform and gives drivers an opportunity to experience speed and power like never before. Powering this beast is a 3.8L V6 Twin-Turbocharged petrol engine that generates 570Ps of power along with 637Nm of torque. The GT-R is fitted with a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission that takes the car from 0-10 kmph in about 3 seconds. Although the GT-R from Nissan looks like a muscle car, it is quite speedy and can reach speeds of up to 315 kmph with ease. The Nissan GTR is a no-brainer option when it comes to sports cars in the Indian market and is one of the perfect choices for interested buyers who want speed and muscle in a car.
